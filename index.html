Hi there! I'm a PhD candidate at Robotics Innovatory, Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea, under the supervision of Prof.Hyouk Ryeol Choi

My research is mainly focused on robotic grasping and manipulation. I'm interested in developing algorithms that enable robots to autonomously manipulate objects in unstructured environments and physically interact with their surroundings.
